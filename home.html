Text-to-Speech Story Generator

Welcome to your very own Text-to-Speech Story Generator! This interactive application lets you type or paste any story and listen to it read aloud. Whether you're learning, relaxing, or entertaining friends — it’s the perfect tool to bring your words to life.

Key Features:

  • Listen to your favorite stories with clear, realistic voices.
  • Choose different voice styles and adjust speaking speed.
  • Pause and resume your story whenever you wish.
  • Great for readers, students, and anyone who loves stories.
